#### Nickname
_Lamer (from the English lame — “crippled, limping”) is a computer slang term for a person who is bad at using a computer._

I gave myself this nickname when I was living in a dorm with no internet access. To borrow Wi-Fi from a neighbor, I started exploring different capabilities of one well-known Linux distribution. While consuming guides, I realized that you cannot truly call yourself knowledgeable about computers if you do not understand how they work deep inside, and if you do not know networking, hardware, and the terminal. So after taking this nickname, I kept developing in that direction. One way or another, all of my nicknames contain this word.
